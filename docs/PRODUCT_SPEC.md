# Product Spec

## Product

Lead.AI WhatsApp Agent

## Role

WhatsApp AI assistant for business leads and customer support.

## Value Proposition

A WhatsApp automation agent that answers common questions, qualifies leads, and routes high-value conversations to humans.

## Problem

Businesses miss WhatsApp leads and manually answer repetitive questions.

## Target Users

- Local service businesses
- Retail stores
- Appointment-based businesses
- Support teams
- Agencies

## Core Features

- WhatsApp message handling
- Lead qualification
- FAQ responses
- Booking intent detection
- Business hours handling
- Escalation to human
- CRM-ready lead storage
- Twilio integration placeholder

## MVP Scope

The MVP should prove one practical workflow before expanding into a full product.

First valuable demo: Implement webhook verification and a simple qualification conversation flow.

## Out Of Scope For MVP

- Production claims.
- Real customer data.
- Complex multi-tenant enterprise controls.
- Unsupported accuracy claims.
- Paid billing flows unless explicitly required for the first demo.

## Success Metrics

- A visitor understands the product in under 10 seconds.
- The primary workflow can be demonstrated with safe sample data.
- Setup and security expectations are documented.
- AI outputs include limitations, explanations, or review guidance where relevant.
